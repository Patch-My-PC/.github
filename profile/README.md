# Patch My PC – Smart Update Automation & Security Patching Tool

**Patch My PC** is a powerful, flexible patch management solution designed to help individuals and teams keep applications updated, reduce vulnerabilities, and maintain system compliance.  
With **Patch My PC**, you can transform chaotic manual updates into automated, scheduled workflows.  
**Patch My PC** supports silent patching, inventory tracking, and offline deployment, making it an essential security companion for IT professionals, small businesses, and home users.

## About Patch My PC

**Patch My PC** combines the simplicity of a software updater with the power of an enterprise patch manager. Unlike basic update tools, **Patch My PC** offers deep customization: custom schedules, exclusion lists, pre/post scripts, and rollback points.  
**Patch My PC** works seamlessly with your existing infrastructure (on-premises, cloud, hybrid) and provides real-time reports across all endpoints.  
Whether you manage five devices or five thousand, **Patch My PC** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**Patch My PC** respects your privacy – all data stays under your control with optional air-gapped mode.  
With **Patch My PC**, you gain clarity, reduce patch fatigue, and secure every application daily.
